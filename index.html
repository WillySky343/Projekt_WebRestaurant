Our Story

A Family Passion Turned into Art

La Bella Cucina was founded in 2005 by the Rossi family, who brought the authentic flavours of their native Tuscany to Warsaw. What started as a small trattoria has grown into one of the city's most beloved dining destinations.

Our kitchen is led by Head Chef Marco Rossi, who blends traditional recipes passed down through generations with contemporary techniques to create dishes that celebrate the very best of Italian gastronomy.
